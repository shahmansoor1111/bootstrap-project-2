What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. It enables machines to learn, reason, and solve problems, making them smarter and more efficient.

Fields of AI

Machine Learning

Enables systems to learn and improve from experience without explicit programming.

Natural Language Processing

Helps machines understand, interpret, and respond to human language.

Computer Vision

Allows machines to recognize, interpret, and process visual data from the world.

Robotics

Combines AI with machines to perform tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously.
