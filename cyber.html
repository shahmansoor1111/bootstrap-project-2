Cyber Security

Protecting data, networks, and systems from digital attacks

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It is crucial to protect sensitive information and ensure safe digital experiences.

Key Areas of Cyber Security

Network Security

Protecting networks from intrusions, malware, and unauthorized access.

Application Security

Securing software applications by detecting vulnerabilities and applying best practices.

Information Security

Ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.

Cloud Security

Protecting cloud-based systems and data from cyber threats.

Technologies & Tools

  • Firewalls & VPNs
  • Antivirus & Anti-malware
  • Penetration Testing Tools
  • SIEM (Security Information & Event Management)
  • Encryption & Hashing
  • Identity & Access Management
